# Pumpkin Spice Latte

## Pumpkin Spice Mix

Makes ~3 Tbsp

| Spice           | Amount |
| --------------- | ------ |
| Ground cinnamon | 2 Tbsp |
| Ground ginger   | 2 tsp  |
| Ground nutmeg   | 2 tsp  |
| Ground allspice | 1 tsp  |
| Ground cloves   | 1 tsp  |

Whisk together and store in an airtight jar.


## Pumpkin Spice Latte Recipe

1. Warm 1 cup of milk (dairy or oat is ideal) in a small pot for milk frother.
1. Add 1/2 to 1 tsp of [pumpkin pie spice mix](pumpkin_spice_mix.md)
1. Add 1â€“2 tsp maple syrup or brown sugar.
1. Optional: Add 1-2 tsp canned pumpkin puree
1. Froth or whisk until steamy and combined.
1. Pour over espresso or strong coffee.
1. Optional: pinch of spice mix on top + whipped cream.
